About neversleep.

neversleep. is a rising metalcore duo from Youngstown, Ohio pushing the boundaries of emotional intensity and sonic weight. Vocalist Mason Edmunds and guitarist/producer Alan Bryan craft haunting, high-energy tracks that blend metalcore aggression with emo and post-hardcore vulnerability. Their music explores themes of isolation, betrayal, and inner turmoil—wrapped in cinematic production and visceral lyricism.

What sets neversleep. apart is their versatility. Taking a “write what you love” approach has ensured that their music remains authentic and deeply personal while providing a sonic experience that can appeal to a wider audience. Tracks like “Christmas Gives Me A Stomachache” draw listeners into a raw emo-rock narrative steeped in introspective sorrow and loss, while songs such as “Prototype” unleash a brutal onslaught of low-tuned guitars, and unyielding intensity, channeling the chaos of a dystopian nightmare.

This dynamic range allows neversleep. to explore the full spectrum of emotional and tonal intensity—from quiet reflection to raw, visceral expression.
